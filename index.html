Welcome from Andrew

WOC is a new inclusive choir for anyone aged (roughly) 70 or over. We welcome everyone, regardless of singing ability and actively encourage anyone who loves music, or might gain pleasure from being around music and friendly faces to attend. We are wheelchair accessible – family members and friends also welcome to come and get involved. Submit your email below to register your interest in joining the choir!

  • About
  • Andrew is deputy organist at St Mary's Church Wimbledon, where he lives with his family. In his spare time he enjoys running on Wimbledon Common. The choir's aim is to enable older people to sing together in a friendly and supportive atmospshere. It is open to absolutely everyone aged over 70 - no previous experience of choral singing is required. If you have further questions, or are interested in joining us, don't hesitate to contact us on andrew@wimbledonoldieschoir.co.uk.
